About the Application

Measure Resolution Time analyzes the content of an issue report and estimates how long it may take to be resolved based on linguistic characteristics such as relevance, tone, toxicity, and comment length. The system uses statistical models derived from public real issue‑tracking datasets to generate an estimated resolution time and highlight parts of the text that may affect responsiveness.

To use the tool, enter an issue title and an initial issue comment into the form on the Calculator page. After submitting, the system will display an estimated resolution time along with recommendations for improving clarity, tone, and relevance. These suggestions are intended to help you revise your issue before posting it to platforms like GitHub. You can adjust your text and resubmit as many times as you like to see how different writing choices influence the prediction.

This application does not store or share any issue titles or comments you submit. All text is processed temporarily for the purpose of generating predictions and recommendations, and no user‑provided content is saved, logged, or used for any secondary purpose. Your inputs remain private and are discarded once the prediction is complete.
